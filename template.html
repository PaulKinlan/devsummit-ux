Always use a viewport

The most critical element for mobile!

Device width:

<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width" />

Initial scale:

<meta name="viewport" content="initial-scale=1.0" />
<meta name="viewport"
  content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0" />
