Tap Targets

Minimum target Recommendations

  • iOS: 44 pts
  • Android: 48 dips
  • Microsoft: 23 DLG

But what do they mean in web terms?

  • iOS: 1 pt (1/76th of an inch)
  • Android: 1 dips (1px at 160dpi)
  • Microsoft: 1 DLG (Who knows?)

Padding is part of the tappable area.

Quite easy when you have a viewport.

Tap Targets - In the real world

  • Increase target size (7mm)
  • Increase margin (5mm minimum)
  • Increase padding

You can actually get away with smaller.
